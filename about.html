About Juste Elvin

Who is Juste Elvin?

Juste Elvin is a #Burundi National with an avid passion for Graphic/Motion Designing in general and he is specialising in Digital Designing.

At present he is a freelance Designer devoted in logo,websites,visual identities and branding and currently working from home. He enjoys communicating (in informal way ) with the client in the advance of the project to have a good anthousiam around so that they can come out with the best possible design solution.
On this site you will find good Graphic Artworks and many related features some done for fun others for education purpose,others are business oriented.
